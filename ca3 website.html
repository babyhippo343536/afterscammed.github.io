Actions to Take After Getting Scammed

You may be having a tough time after getting scammed, but taking these actions can improve your chances of getting back your losses.

Immediate Steps

  1. Do not engage further with the scammer.
  2. Report the scam to Scam Alert SG.
  3. Contact your bank to freeze your account if money was transferred.
  4. Change your passwords if personal information was compromised.
  5. Use ScamShield to block scam messages and calls.
  6. Submit evidence to i-Witness (SPF).
  7. Watch YouTube guides on avoiding scams.

Important Definitions

Phishing
A scam where attackers impersonate legitimate institutions to steal personal data.
Social Engineering
Manipulating individuals into divulging confidential information.
